Research Problem

Modern cyber threats require adaptive systems that address:

  • Evolving attack vectors: Zero-day exploits bypassing signature-based detection
  • High false positives: Existing systems generate excessive alerts (EPA QA Handbook, 2023)
  • Skill gaps: 68% of security teams lack ML expertise (NIST SP 800-181)

Our Solution

HybridML architecture diagram

Combines LSTM networks (temporal patterns), Random Forest (feature importance), and SVM (high-dim classification) with dynamic ensemble weighting.
