Collaboration Process

Workflow Requirements

  • 1
    Team Access: All members must have edit access to website files
  • 2
    Document Review: Team must discuss all documents before posting
  • 3
    Undergraduate Links: Clearly link their documents with proper labeling
  • 4
    Timely Updates: Documents must be posted by deadlines
